Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar

FOUNDING FATHER OF MODERN INDIA (M.A., PH.D., M.SC., D.SC., BARRISTER-AT-LAW, L.L.D., D.LITT)

Datewise important Life events of Babasaheb.

  • 1891 - Born at Mahu (Madhya Pradesh), the fourteenth child of Subhedar Ramji Sakpal and Mrs Bhimabai Ambedkar
  • 1896 - Death of the mother, Mrs Bhimabai Ambedkar
  • 1904 - Entered the Elphinstone High School at Bombay
  • 1906 - Married Ramabai daughter of Mr. Bhiku Walangkar, one of the relations of Gopal Baba Walangkar
  • 1908 - Honoured in a meeting presided over by Shri S K Bole, Shri K A (Dada) Keluskar Guruji presented a book on the life of Gautam Buddha written by him. Entered the Elphinstone College, Bombay
  • 1913 - Death of father Subhedar Ramji Maloji Ambedkar at Bombay
  • 1924 - Started practice in the Bombay High Court.
  • 1927 - Started Satyagraha at Mahad (Dist Kolaba) to secure to the untouchables the Right of access to the Chavdar Tank
  • 1928 - Introduced the “Vatan Bill” in the Bombay Legislative Council.
  • 1932 - Member joint Parliamentary Committee on the Indian Constitutional Reform.
  • 1956 - Cremation at Dadar Chawpatti – Now known as Chaitya Bhoomi
